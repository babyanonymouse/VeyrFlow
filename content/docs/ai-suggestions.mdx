## What the Insights panel shows

The Daily Focus/Insights area highlights helpful suggestions such as:

- Habit ideas to reinforce weak spots
- Focus blocks for high-priority tasks
- Small tweaks to make your routine easier to keep

## When suggestions are generated

- Suggestions are refreshed on a daily cycle.
- If recent suggestions already exist, VeyrFlow can reuse cached results (up to about 24 hours) to keep the app fast and reliable.

## Accept, ignore, or adjust suggestions

- **Accept:** Add or follow the suggested change.
- **Ignore:** Skip it and keep your current setup.
- **Adjust:** Edit the suggestion to better match your schedule or energy level.

## Privacy and AI context

- Tasks marked with **privacy mode** are excluded from AI context.
- Sensitive items stay out of suggestion inputs.
- You stay in control of what the AI can use.
