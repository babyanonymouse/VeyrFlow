# Using Habits

## What is a habit in HabitFlow?
A habit is a repeated action you want to keep consistent (usually daily), like a wellness routine or focus behavior.

## Create and save a habit
- Open the **Habits** page.
- Click **New Habit**.
- Add a title and optional description.
- Choose the frequency (daily).
- Click **Create Habit**.

## How tracking and streaks work
- Each time you complete a habit, HabitFlow records that date.
- Your current streak grows when you keep completing on consecutive days.
- If you skip too long, the streak resets.

## Mark habits as done each day
- On the dashboard or Habits page, click **Complete Habit**.
- Once completed for today, it shows as done and cannot be double-counted that day.

## Example habits
```text
Morning walk for 10 minutes
No phone 30 minutes before bed
```
